Welcome

Hello! I wanted to create a webpage for some of my projects and avoid using any JS or any other kind of bloat. Just HTML and CSS.

I'm a penetration tester with a fairly varied background, spanning scattered experience in web dev, big data, finance and even machine learning. I have a degree in finance and statistics. This website acts as a mix of a portfolio, personal blog and playground for various ideas and experiments. There's a fair amount of old content that needs to be uploaded, so stay tuned.

My goals

I'm currently doing a lot of web pentesting, most of it blackbox. While it is fulfilling and interesting, I'd like to move towards appsec in the future. I'm going at it the other way around. Instead of jumping into something like the OSWE right away, I've been tinkering and building applications on my own. Outside the OSCP, certifications tend to teach you a bunch of tricks that you can repeat on the terminal instead of building a strong foundation and a thorough understanding of the system. Depeding on how far I get into an application lifecycle, I might upload it on to github. I don't feel like uploading a quickly hobbled together project I built in a weekend because I wanted to explore some new tech.

Conferences

I usually dable in more local stuff like work talks or local hacker chapters talks but sometimes I take on the big stage as well - having spoken at a private COBCOE conference in front of a crowd of 300. The slides can be found here. I love conferences, both as an atendee and as a speaker.
